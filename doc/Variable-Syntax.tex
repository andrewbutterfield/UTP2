 Well-formed variables have the following syntax:
 \begin{eqnarray*}
    s \in Std && \mbox{``standard'' variables}
 \\ \ell \in Lst && \mbox{list variables}
 \\ g \in Gen &::=&  s | \ell
 \\ R \in Rsv &::=& O | M | S
 \\ d \in Dcr &::=& {}^\cdot | {}' | {}_x | {}^{``}
 \\ v \in Var &::=& \left( g | R [g,\ldots,g] \right)^d
 \end{eqnarray*}
